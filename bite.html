Bite is a mobile application to keep track of your productivity. This application offers an expressive user-interface with the main-target group being children of ages 7-13. Bite was developed in accordance to the Pomodoro technique, a technique that is used to keep track of time spent working as well as time you spent on leisure. The application is engaging with features such as "Throw your apple in the trash can" through which besides offering a playful UI, it also encourages anti-littering.
